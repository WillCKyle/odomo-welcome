What it is

1

Odomo is a localized event promotion app.

You can see upcoming events and, uniquely, recent events from your local area and around the world. These recent event posts contain user-generated videos and photos alongside monetizable product listings.

2

The unique value proposition is based on a new FOMO-based feature to collect the photos and videos taken at events.

After a few days of exclusivity, this repository of user-generated media from a recent event is published to the app feed. Everyone can open this recent event post to see people’s memories from the event and ads by the event host.

3

Odomo generates tangible value for various community stakeholders.

Hosts can promote their events after they pass and gain a new audience for their products. Everyone else gets a first-person view what’s going on in the world around them, and they can find photos of themselves or great moments recorded by other people who attended the same event.

What the party gains

1

A scalable source of revenue.

The primary sources of revenue from Odomo are platform fees for selling event tickets and ad spaces sold to the businesses hosting events. Once businesses are brought onto the platform, they generate passive revenue. The market size is large and operational costs remain low.

2

A flexible business opportunity for chapters.

Chapters will receive a unique invite link to give to businesses, and the chapter will receive ~80% of the revenue from that business. This allows chapters to approach their local businesses and political organizations with a win-win offer to provide immediate value and earn revenue. This also creates a decentralized marketing strategy for Odomo.

3

A platform for dual-power construction.

As Odomo gains popularity, the party can use it as a base for further party building. First, the party builds its authority by contributing to local economic development. Second, the platform will build and provide us access to a network of local stakeholders, allowing us to research and develop more initiatives to support our communities.
